About Us

The Helping Others Smile Foundation is a non-profit organization created by Manav Rungta, Manasi Rungta, and Manya Rungta in 2017.

We invest in people in order to make a difference in the lives of the current generation and provide a better future for upcoming generations. We envision creating a more happy society by sharing knowledge and doing something far-reaching for the community.

Manav Rungta

Co-Founder

Believing that meaningful change begins with service, Manav has devoted himself to growing Helping Others Smile Foundation into a catalyst for community impact. Through his leadership, the organization develops sustainable initiatives in education, healthcare, and volunteerism that empower individuals, strengthen communities, and inspire others to give back.

Manasi Rungta

Co-Founder

Passionate about fostering holistic well-being and community empowerment, Manasi has been instrumental in shaping the foundation's core initiatives. Her visionary approach to outreach and her dedication to compassionate service have enabled the organization to create lasting, positive change, ensuring that every individual they touch feels supported and valued.

Manya Rungta

Co-Founder

Driven by a genuine desire to uplift her community, Manya co-founded the Helping Others Smile Foundation to build a brighter future across generations. Today, she puts that mission into practice by teaching technology classes to seniors and leading English lessons for beginners. Her focus is on breaking down communication barriers and giving people the practical skills they need to stay confident and connected.

Our Projects

Discover the initiatives we lead to create a more knowledgeable and happier society.

💻

Technology Class

Empowering individuals through essential technology education.

We provide hands-on classes and instructional videos/manuals to help seniors and underprivileged communities bridge the digital divide. From using smartphones to making video calls with loved ones, we ensure technology is accessible to everyone.

📖

Tutoring in India

Providing educational support and guidance to students in India.

Through dedicated virtual sessions, we connect with students in India to provide them with essential tutoring in various subjects. We aim to equip them with the knowledge and confidence they need to succeed in their academic journeys.

🌱

Greenify the World

Environmental initiatives aimed at promoting sustainability.

Our environmental branch focuses on actionable ways to protect our planet. We organize community cleanups, tree planting drives, and educational seminars to spread awareness on climate change and sustainability.

👴

Helping Seniors

Programs designed to support and bring joy to senior citizens.

We spend quality time with senior citizens, teaching them how to use technology to stay connected with their families, playing games, and ensuring they feel valued and less lonely in their communities.

❤️

Volunteer Leadership

MD Anderson Volunteer Leadership and Spiritual Workshops.

We take pride in our leadership roles at MD Anderson, coordinating volunteer efforts. We also host spiritual workshops to promote mental well-being, mindfulness, and personal growth among our volunteers and community members.

🎵

Musical Evenings

Community gatherings and cultural events to bring people together.

We organize and host musical evenings, such as our special event with Anup Jalota. These cultural gatherings aim to unite the community, celebrate art, and raise funds for our various philanthropic initiatives.
